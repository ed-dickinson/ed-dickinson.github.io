Will Jenkinson, Projection, 2016

Will Jenkinson

‘It's hard to think about landscape without technology. It's ever present. A phone in front of you or a plane in the sky. For me they have become synonymous with each other’

Will Jenkinson is a painter and furniture designer. His latest work explores how landscape, memory and technology are linked through the ubiquitous presence of the smartphone camera. His paintings are abstract, but the starting point is always the captured image.
