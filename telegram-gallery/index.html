Please note Telegram Gallery is currently dormant

Telegram is a non profit gallery and small press supporting emerging painters, photographers and printmakers.

In protest against the rising cost of rents, we began holding exhibitions in borrowed spaces in London back in 2014. Now the gallery operates mostly online.

We also run the popular #telegramtakeover series on instagram, inviting visual artists of all disciplines to take over our account every week and show their work to our followers.
