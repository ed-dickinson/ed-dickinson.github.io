Tumbleword

🕹️ Tumbleword is a browser-based game I made that combines elements of Tetris and Wordle. You place falling letters to find a 5-letter word pre-selected by the computer. The speed increases as you successfully find words and clear previous letters. There is a leaderboard impemented that logs the 10 highest scores across all users.

[The front-end is made with vanilla JS, and the leaderboard is an API served by Node.js and express.js.]
