Abilities

Javascript is where my heart and my focus lies. I spend most of my time making websites using either JS frameworks for their flexibility and speed of use, or vanilla javascript, for the exact customisation and specificity, and the simple pleasure of building something from the ground up.

Being as fond as I am of Javascript, it seems only natural that I would choose it for the back-end as well, and I cherish the ability to program the back-end and front-end in the same language, for the consistent mindset it enables and the architectural unity.

I won't lie, I'm not head over heels in love with React, but I do value it as a tool in it's speed of website creation and amazing ability to display endless data.

What would any website be without the bread and butter of the front-end? I use it with the respect it deserves.

MongoDB: How else to round out the MERN stack and put that feather in my cap?

I favor Heroku for it's simplicity in hosting an API, but I also use AWS, especially for Elastic Beanstalk's rapid hosting.

I'm a big fan of the JAM stack, and I think the split architure gives way to incredible versatility, cross-usability and re-usability.

I'm a compulsive saver and versioner, so Git and GitHub are indispensible to me.

I'm no Wordpress master, but I know my way around it, and find it a good way to give customer's a blog-based website, as a bespoke CMS takes time.
